# Day 10 – Advanced Search Techniques

## Overview
This practical focuses on comparing different search engines and OSINT tools to investigate the public digital footprint of Zoom Communications.

## Learning Objectives
- Compare search results from Google, Bing, and Shodan.
- Understand the strengths of different search engines.
- Identify publicly available information ethically.
