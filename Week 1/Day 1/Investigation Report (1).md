

Investigation Report – OpenAI

## Company Name:
OpenAI is an artificial intelligence (AI) research and technology company that
develops advanced AI models and tools to benefit individuals, businesses, and
society.
## Company Type:
OpenAI is an artificial intelligence (AI) research and technology
company that focuses on developing advanced AI models, tools, and
applications for individuals, businesses, and developers.
## Founded
OpenAI was founded in December 2015 with the goal of ensuring that
artificial intelligence benefits all of humanity. The organization was
established to conduct cutting-edge AI research while promoting the safe
and responsible development of AI technologies. Since its founding,
OpenAI has become one of the leading organizations in the field of
artificial intelligence.
## Founders
OpenAI was founded by Sam Altman, Elon Musk, Greg Brockman,
Ilya Sutskever, Wojciech Zaremba, and John Schulman. These
founders brought together expertise in technology, research, and
entrepreneurship to advance artificial intelligence. Their vision was to
create AI systems that are safe, reliable, and beneficial for people around
the world


## Headquarters
OpenAI is headquartered in San Francisco, California, United States.
The headquarters serves as the company's main center for research,
innovation, and the development of advanced artificial intelligence
technologies. From this location, OpenAI collaborates with researchers,
engineers, and partners to create safe and beneficial AI solutions.
## CEO
The Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of OpenAI is Sam Altman.
Under his leadership, OpenAI has developed several advanced AI
models and expanded its research and products globally. He plays a key
role in guiding the company's vision of developing artificial intelligence
that benefits all of humanity.
Products and Services
OpenAI develops a wide range of artificial intelligence products and
services. Its major products include ChatGPT, GPT-5.5, AI APIs for
developers, image generation models, and voice and multimodal AI
tools. These products help individuals, businesses, researchers, and
developers improve productivity, automate tasks, and create innovative
AI-powered applications.
## Official Website
The official website of OpenAI is OpenAI. The website provides
detailed information about the company's research, products, services,
latest updates, career opportunities, and developer resources. It is the
primary source of official information about the organization.


## Social Media
OpenAI maintains an active presence on several social media platforms
to share updates, research, and announcements. Its official accounts
include X (@OpenAI), LinkedIn, and YouTube. These platforms help
the company engage with the global AI community and provide
information about its latest developments and innovations.
## Mission
OpenAI's mission is to develop artificial intelligence that benefits all of
humanity. The organization is committed to creating safe, reliable, and
responsible AI technologies that improve people's lives and support
scientific and technological progress. It also focuses on ensuring that the
benefits of artificial intelligence are shared widely across society.
## Conclusion
OpenAI is one of the world's leading AI research and technology
companies. It develops advanced AI models such as ChatGPT and GPT,
provides AI tools for individuals and businesses, and focuses on building
AI responsibly and safely.
