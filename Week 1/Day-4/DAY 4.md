

## DAY 4
Digital Footprint Investigation of Swiggy

## Objective
The objective of this practical session was to investigate the
digital footprint of Swiggy using publicly available information
available on the internet. The investigation focused on collecting
information about the company's official website, social media
presence, online identity, metadata, public information, and
digital reputation by applying Open Source Intelligence
(OSINT) techniques while following ethical and legal
guidelines.

## Introduction
A digital footprint is the collection of information that an
individual or organization leaves on the internet through
websites, social media platforms, online publications, business
directories, blogs, and news articles. Every online activity
contributes to an organization's digital identity and public
reputation.
In this practical session, Swiggy was selected as the target
organization to understand how companies build and maintain

their online presence. The investigation involved analyzing
publicly available information from multiple trusted sources to
evaluate Swiggy's digital footprint and online visibility.
