# Day 4 – Digital Footprint Investigation

## 📌 Overview
This practical focused on investigating the digital footprint of **Swiggy** using publicly available information. The investigation covered the company's website, social media presence, online identity, news coverage, and public information using ethical OSINT techniques.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Understand digital footprints.
- Investigate an organization's online presence.
- Analyze websites and social media.
- Collect publicly available information ethically.
