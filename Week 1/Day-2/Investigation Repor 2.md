

## Investigation Report – Wikipedia

## Website Name
Wikipedia is a free online encyclopedia that provides information on a
wide range of topics and is created and maintained by volunteers
worldwide.
## Purpose
The main purpose of Wikipedia is to provide free access to knowledge
and information for people around the world. It allows users to read and
contribute articles on various subjects.
## Founder
Wikipedia was founded by Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger. Their goal
was to create a free and collaborative online encyclopedia that anyone
could access and edit. They believed that knowledge should be freely
available to everyone, regardless of location or background. Since its
launch, Wikipedia has grown into one of the world's largest online
information resources, with millions of articles available in multiple
languages. The platform is maintained and continuously improved by a
global community of volunteer contributors, making it one of the most
successful collaborative projects on the internet.
## Launch Date
Wikipedia was officially launched on January 15, 2001. It was created as
a free online encyclopedia that allows users to create, edit, and share
knowledge collaboratively. Since its launch, Wikipedia has grown

rapidly and become one of the most visited websites on the internet.
Today, it contains millions of articles in multiple languages and serves as
a valuable source of information for students, researchers, educators, and
the general public around the world
## Headquarters
Wikipedia is operated by the Wikimedia Foundation, which is
headquartered in San Francisco, California, United States. The
Wikimedia Foundation is a non-profit organization responsible for
supporting Wikipedia and several other free knowledge projects. It
manages the technical infrastructure, ensures the website remains
accessible, and works to promote free access to knowledge worldwide.
## Owner / Organization
Wikipedia is owned and managed by the Wikimedia Foundation, a non-
profit organization that supports free knowledge projects around the
world. The foundation provides financial, technical, and administrative
support to Wikipedia and its sister projects. It is funded mainly through
public donations and is committed to making educational content freely
available to everyone. Through its efforts, millions of people around the
world can access reliable information without any subscription fees.
## Conclusion
Wikipedia is one of the world's largest online knowledge platforms. It
provides free access to information and operates through a collaborative
editing model. The website uses secure HTTPS communication, a robust
DNS infrastructure, and scalable architecture to serve millions of users
globally.
