## Analysis
## Objective
- Understand the structure of domain names and the different types
of Top-Level Domains (TLDs), including generic TLDs (gTLDs)
and country-code TLDs (ccTLDs).
- Learn how WHOIS records provide publicly available domain
registration information.
- Identify key domain registration details such as the registrar,
creation date, expiration date, and registration status.
- Compare domains that use WHOIS privacy protection with those
that publicly display registrant information.
- Develop practical OSINT skills by analyzing publicly available
domain registration data using WHOIS lookup tools.
- Understand the importance of WHOIS information in domain
intelligence and cybersecurity investigations

## Best Practices
- Use WHOIS information only for legitimate and ethical
investigations.
- Respect privacy-protected registrations.
- Verify information using multiple trusted WHOIS sources.
- Do not misuse registrant information.
- Follow legal and organizational guidelines during OSINT
investigations
## Conclusion
- The Day 13 practical provided hands-on experience in analyzing
domain registration information using WHOIS services. By
examining different domain types and comparing privacy-
protected and publicly available registrations, a better
understanding of domain ownership, registrars, and TLDs was
developed. This knowledge forms an important foundation for

infrastructure-focused OSINT investigations and supports the
analysis of an organization's online presence.
